ABOUT

Transcom Consumer Products Limited (TCPL) is PepsiCo’s first snacks franchise in the world. TCPL spreads the goodness of world-standard PepsiCo snacks across Bangladesh, produced at the state-of-the-art plant in Bogura. TCPL manufactures a range of lip-smacking flavors of Kurkure such as Cream and Onion, Spicy Chicken among others, packages Quaker Oats and also makes world’s no. 01 snacks brand Lay’s available to consumers.

Poised to be the Leader of Bangladesh’s
Snacks Industry

Products

Transcom Consumer Products Ltd., as PepsiCo's first snacks franchise in the world, is poised to lead the market with its global brands.

Kurkure

Kurkure entered the snacking scene in Bangladesh in 2014 with Tok Misti Jhal and Chilli Chatka followed by Masala Munch soon after. Currently, we have 5 exciting flavors in our portfolio: Cream & Onion, Naughty Tomato, Spicy Chicken, Chilli Chatka, and Masala Munch.

Lay’s

The world’s largest snack brand has proven to be a fan favorite in Bangladesh. Currently, Transcom Consumer Products Ltd. has globally popular flavors of Lay’s like American Style Cream and Onion, Spanish Tomato Tango etc. TCPL has taken the drive to serve the Bangladeshi consumer the world’s favorite potato chips with the aim to bring in an array of exciting flavors in the near future

Quaker

With a heritage of 135 years, Quaker entered the Bangladeshi market in 2014 to spread the goodness of Oats. With the call to action, “Start Something Good”, Quaker intends to make it’s way into Bangladesh’s diet and be a partner in healthy living with it’s unique trait in providing long lasting energy, fiber, and an ideal meal for weight control.

Technical Expertise

TCPL is committed to maintaining the highest standard of quality. Its dedicated facility, state-of-the-art equipment, skilled workforce, strict adherence to compliance, safety and a healthy workplace have made it a truly a world-class facility.World renowned AIB International has given a high score 925 of 1000 for Food Safety and Hygiene for our Bogura Plant. We have consistently scored 900+ multiple times from the inception of our operation.
