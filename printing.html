TRANSCRAFT

ABOUT

Transcraft is a high-speed printing company focused on newspaper and commercial packaging printing. Transcraft is the largest of its kind in Bangladesh, as a newspaper printer, with facilities in Tejgaon, Dhaka; Kalurghat, Chattogram and Fuldighi, Bagura.

The Company initiated its operations by printing for the leading Bangla newspaper in Bangladesh, The Daily Prothom Alo, and the largest English-language newspaper The Daily Star. Transcraft’s diverse capabilities include many forms such as newspapers, magazines, and books. Additionally, Transcraft has endeavored into new segments of the printing industry with expansion into commercial printing and packaging at its new site in Kanchpur, Narayanganj. This will allow Transcraft to be the prime solution provider for high-quality printing to meet a plethora of commercial printing needs, such as food packaging, pharmaceutical packaging, and other commercial packaging variants.

Printing Excellence

As a newspaper printer, our success is reflected in the satisfaction of our valued clients and the trust they keep in us for the last 22 years. Our timely services are aimed to reach readers and clients on time, every day, with the highest quality printing and services possible.

With that success and track record in mind, we aim and vision to spread our wings to the boundless opportunities of Packaging printing.

Packaging

Transcraft is all set to get into the diversified, embellished, and creative field of packaging printing.

With brand-new and state-of-the-art offset printing machines, die-cutters, gluers, UV coaters, laminators, and so on, Transcraft is ready to deliver best-in-class packaging solutions in the country.

The printing facility at Kanchpur, Narayanganj, Dhaka with modern machinery and facilities will cater to pharma, foods, electronics packaging, and FMCG needs in Bangladesh.
