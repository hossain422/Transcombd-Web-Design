BANGLADESH LAMPS

ABOUT

Bangladesh Lamps Ltd. (BLL) is Transcom’s only publicly listed company, established in 1962. In 1993, Transcom took ownership of BLL through the acquisition of Philips’ operation in Bangladesh. The shares of the company are publicly traded in Dhaka and Chittagong Stock Exchange.

BLL manufactures a full range of lighting products starting from GLS to all LED lighting products under the Transtec Brand in its ISO-certified production facility located in Narsingdi. BLL has also ventured into new segments of the lighting and electrical industries by developing and manufacturing products such as Smart Bulbs and Electrical Accessories. BLL markets its products through its countrywide distribution network of over 80,000 electrical and grocery outlets.
