ESKAYEF
PHARMACEUTICALS

ABOUT

Eskayef Pharmaceuticals Ltd. is one of the leaders in Bangladesh’s pharmaceutical industry. With its presence across the world, it has earned coveted accreditations from the US FDA, UK MHRA, EU EMA, TGA Australia, and more. Eskayef spread its wings in 1990 when the Bangladeshi operations of SmithKline & French were acquired by Transcom Group.

Eskayef prides itself on being a trusted manufacturer of top-quality medicine for Bangladesh and the world.

Milestones

1979

Multinational SmithKline & French (SK&F) enters Bangladesh

1990

Transcom acquires SmithKline & French (SK&F) and becomes Eskayef Bangladesh Ltd

2017

Eskayef Bangladesh Ltd. emerges as Eskayef Pharmaceuticals Ltd. to go more global
